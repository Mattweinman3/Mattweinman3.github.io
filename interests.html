Interests

Scuba Diving

I got my initial scuba certification in 2007 at the age of 11. From there I preceeded to get my Advanced Open Water Certification in 2012. Scuba Diving has been a passion of mine for almost ten years now and it will be for years to come.

Backpacking and Camping

I did not grow up in a family were camping or being in nature was very common, however, once in college I started to realize I had a passion for the outdoors. Since being in college I have done a 53 mile backpacking trip on a Pennslyvanian strech of the Appilacian Trail and founded a major campus wide Outdoors Club.

Rock Climbing

After my football and baseball careers ended in high school due to injuries, I took up rock climbing. Climbing is a great way for me to stay in shape (physically and mentally), as well as a way for me to, once again, have the outdoors as an important part of my life.
